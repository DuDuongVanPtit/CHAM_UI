Usage Guidelines

Do's

  • Use deep blue for primary actions and headers
  • Maintain consistent spacing and padding
  • Use accent colors purposefully for feedback
  • Keep icons outline style with 2px stroke

Don'ts

  • Don't use colors outside the defined palette
  • Don't mix different icon styles
  • Don't use red for anything other than errors
  • Don't compromise on accessibility standards
